GT Road. Ghaziabad.
Before the Car Market Existed.

"Madhuban was already a name this city knew — long before the car accessories market on GT Road became what it is today. We were here first. We watched it all grow around us."

Vivek Paruthi — Bitoo Bhaiya to everyone in the market — has run this shop on one principle since day one: source it right, install it right, stand behind your work. No shortcuts. No inflated prices. The reputation didn't come from advertising. It came from 35 years of getting it right.

Today Madhuban Motors serves customers from across Delhi NCR — Ghaziabad, Noida, Delhi, Bulandshahr, and beyond. But the shop is still exactly where it always was. Same GT Road. Same standard.
